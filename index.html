  • Starting a vertical or hanging garden can be a fun and space-saving way to bring greenery into your home. Here's how you can start:

    • Choose plants that are suitable for vertical or hanging gardens (e.g., pothos, ferns, succulents, ivy).
    • Use planters, vertical racks, or shelves to hold the plants. Make sure to use pots with good drainage.
    • Install hooks or rails to hang small baskets or containers for plants like trailing vines or smaller flowers.
    • Ensure that your plants get enough light by placing them in areas with indirect sunlight.
    • Water the plants carefully and make sure the soil is not too damp to avoid overwatering.

    Vertical and hanging gardens are perfect for small spaces, balconies, or even indoor walls that need a touch of greenery!
